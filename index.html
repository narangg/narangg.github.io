About me

I am a PhD student in the School of Electrical Engineering and Computer Science at Washington State University.

My general research interest is in using ML for dynamic resource management for emerging architectures inlcuding CPU-GPU, ReRAMs.

My current research is motivated from sustainable computing for AI/ML. Specific topics include:

  • Resource management in Large manycore systems using Voltage/Frequency Islands
  • ML methods such as Bayesian optimization, Imitation learning, Reinforcement learning
  • Uncertainty aware Online learning for Dynamic resource management in Large manycore systems
  • Emerging domain specific architectures for CNN and other deep learning workloads

Before PhD, I got my Masters in Technology (M.Tech.) degree in VLSI and Embedded Systems from IIIT DELHI, New Delhi.

I am very fortunate to work with my advisor Prof. Partha Pratim Pande and Dr. Jana Doppa
